Our story

Established in 1990, RSk Fashion carries forward the prestigious legacy of Thilakawardhana Online (Pvt) Ltd, offering a premier shopping experience rooted in over three decades of trust and excellence. Built on the ideology of an accessible department store, we have evolved from a traditional apparel retailer into a diversified destination featuring high-quality accessories, appliances, and mobile devices.

Our core philosophy remains deeply customer-focused, driven by a dedicated staff committed to guiding you through a seamless shopping journey. Beyond retail, RSk Fashion is proud of its heritage in community investment, contributing to the infrastructure of public monuments across Sri Lanka.

By embracing rapidly changing technology through our advanced online platform and active social media presence, we ensure that the best level of quality is always within your reach. Follow us as we continue to integrate tradition with innovation, bringing the community together through superior service and style.
